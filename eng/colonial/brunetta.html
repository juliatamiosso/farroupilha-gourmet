Restaurante Família Brunetta

↩ Back

Homemade food served at the table: salads, traditional Italian hot dishes, grilled sides, and other accompaniments, with unlimited refills, as well as capeletti soup served at the table. Family-friendly environment, spacious, with easy parking. Well-rated service. Above-average prices. Reservation is recommended for dinner.
