Cooperativa Vinícola São João

↩ Back

The São João Winery Cooperative was founded in 1931 by a small group of families who brought with them the traditions and work of their Italian descendants and immigrants. Today, nearly 500 families are associated with the cooperative, responsible for producing over 23 million kilos of grapes, which are transformed into table wines, fine wines, sparkling wines, and juices. It has a retail space for the sale and tasting of a variety of wines and sparkling wines that have been awarded nationally and internationally.
