    Did you know that Farroupilha is the

    National Capital of Moscatel?


    Since 2019, Farroupilha proudly holds the title of National Capital of Moscatel, a recognition of its excellence in the production of this unique and enchanting wine. Thanks to its privileged climate and fertile soil in the Serra Gaúcha, the city has become one of Brazil's main wine-producing hubs, especially in cultivating the Moscato grape, which gives rise to the famous sparkling wine.
